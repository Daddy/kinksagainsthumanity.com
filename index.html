What is Kinks Against Humanity?

An oddly executed, unofficial, and parody expansion card set for Cards Against Humanity™ that the vanillas don't want you to know about. It features 69 black cards and 181 white cards crafted by some of the kinkest motherfuckers in the world.

A portion of our profits will help support charities/organizations including the NCSF (National Coalition for Sexual Freedom), Critters In Need, and many other groups helping make a difference for millions all across the globe.
